About Us

Casa Correa is a secluded resort in Angat, Bulacan. It was established by two sisters who work as nurses in the United Kingdom. They understand the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance and practising self-care, especially with the gruelling work demands of the NHS. Taking a break after a week of hard work is crucial to well-being.

Living a fast-paced city life can be challenging for our overall well-being. Among the challenges are heavy traffic, which can lead to stress; pollution that affects our health; a demanding work culture that impacts our work-life balance; overcrowded living conditions; a high cost of living that causes financial strain; social isolation despite being surrounded by a dense population, and disparities in healthcare access. To address these issues, we must use a holistic approach focusing on individual levels to mitigate the negative effects on our well-being.

In a shared vision of nurturing well-being, two sisters embarked on a journey to craft a sanctuary where every breath carries tranquillity and every moment resonates with rejuvenation. Inspired by the whispers of nature and the melody of serenity, they envisioned a private resort where the essence of holistic wellness intertwines seamlessly with luxury and hospitality. Together, they set out to create a haven where guests immerse themselves in a tapestry of wellness experiences, weaving together the threads of relaxation, connection, and renewal.

Vision and Mission

At Casa Correa, we envision a tranquil haven nestled in the heart of Angat, Bulacan, where weary souls find solace amidst the serene embrace of nature. Inspired by the ethos of balance and self-care, our founders, two sisters hailing from the demanding world of healthcare in the United Kingdom, have crafted a sanctuary where every moment is an opportunity for rejuvenation and renewal. With a harmonious blend of luxurious amenities and immersive wellness experiences, we invite our guests to embark on a journey of holistic well-being, where the stresses of city life melt away and serenity prevails.

Our mission at Casa Correa is to curate an unparalleled hospitality experience that transcends mere accommodation, offering guests a sanctuary where the pursuit of well-being is woven into every aspect of their stay. With personalised services, thoughtfully designed amenities, and a commitment to hospitality excellence, we strive to empower individuals to prioritise self-care, embrace mindfulness, and cultivate a profound sense of inner peace. We aim to inspire transformative experiences and create cherished memories that linger long after their departure by fostering an environment of tranquillity, connection, and rejuvenation.

At Casa Correa, the lotus flower serves as a profound source of inspiration, symbolizing purity, enlightenment, and resilience. Just as the lotus emerges from the murky depths of the pond to bloom in pristine beauty, our resort embodies the transformative journey of growth and renewal. Like the lotus, we strive to create a sanctuary where guests can rise above the challenges of daily life and find serenity amidst adversity. The lotus's ability to thrive in adverse conditions reflects our belief in the power of resilience and inner strength, guiding guests on a path of self-discovery and personal transformation. With its captivating beauty and timeless symbolism, the lotus flower serves as a beacon of hope and inspiration at Casa Correa, reminding us that even in the darkest of times, there is always the potential for growth, beauty, and renewal.
